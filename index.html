The Artist

A Story Told
in Color

I'm Onab — a multidisciplinary visual artist whose work explores the emotional landscapes of the human experience.

My journey began in the quiet hours of early mornings, filling sketchbooks with fragments of dreams and half-remembered feelings. Over twelve years, those fragments grew into a body of work that spans digital painting, traditional oils, and conceptual illustration.

I work at the intersection of the visceral and the refined — raw emotion distilled into precise, deliberate brushwork. Each piece is a conversation between chaos and control, between what can be felt and what can be seen.

I believe art should disturb the comfortable and comfort the disturbed — it exists to crack open the ordinary and reveal the extraordinary within.
